Biography

Hongyi Li is a Ph.D. student at Xidian University, China, supervised by Professor Yongchao Wang. She is also a visiting Ph.D. student at Emory University since 2020, and her foreign advisor is Professor Liang Zhao. Before that, she received the B.Eng. degree (Hons.) in Telecommunications Engineering from Xidian University and graduated as the valedictorian of Xidian in 2019.
